	EN	Thanks for choosing to publish your playlist definition. The advantage of publishing a playlist definition is that other users can use it and it will also be used for ideas of new functionallity in the Playlist Generator plugin. Publishing a playlist definition is also a great way of improving the functionality in the Playlist Generator plugin by showing the developer what types of playlist definitions you use, besides those already included with the plugin.

PLUGIN_PLAYLISTGENERATOR_REGISTERMESSAGE
	EN	You can choose to publish your playlist definition either anonymously or by registering a user and login. The advantage of registering is that other people will be able to see that you have published the playlist definition, you will get credit for it and you will also be sure that no one else can update or change your published playlist definition. The e-mail adress will only be used to contact you if I have some questions to you regarding one of your data queries, it will not show up on any web pages. If you already have registered a user, just hit the Login button.

PLUGIN_PLAYLISTGENERATOR_LOGINMESSAGE
	EN	You can choose to publish your playlist definition either anonymously or by registering a user and login. The advantage of registering is that other people will be able to see that you have published the playlist definition, you will get credit for it and you will also be sure that no one else can update or change your published playlist definition. Hit the &quot;Register &amp; Login&quot; button if you have not previously registered.

PLUGIN_PLAYLISTGENERATOR_PUBLISHMESSAGE_DESCRIPTION
	EN	It is important that you enter a good description of your playlist definition, describe what your playlist definition do and if it is based on one of the existing data queries it is a good idea to mention this and describe which extensions you have made. <br><br>It is also a good idea to try to make the &quot;Unique identifier&quot; as uniqe as possible as this will be used for filename when downloading the playlist definition. This is especially important if you have choosen to publish your playlist definition anonymously as it can easily be overwritten if the identifier is not unique. Please try to not use spaces and language specific characters in the unique identifier since these could cause problems on some operating systems.
